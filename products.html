Our Products

Simple and Powered

Explore the fundamentals of mechanical systems in "Simple and Powered Machines," where you'll learn to design, build, and optimize both basic and advanced mechanical devices. This hands-on course combines theory with practical skills to empower your understanding of engineering principles.

XL Duplo's

Discover the world of XL Duplo's in this engaging course, where you'll master advanced techniques for building and designing with large-scale blocks. Perfect for enhancing creativity and structural skills in a fun, hands-on environment.

WeDo 2.0

Dive into the exciting world of robotics with the WeDo 2.0 course, where you'll build and program interactive models using LEGO's user-friendly technology. Perfect for beginners, this course fosters creativity and problem-solving skills through hands-on learning.

Arduino Robot

Unlock the potential of robotics with our Arduino Robot course, where you'll learn to design, build, and program robots using Arduino technology. This course blends electronics and coding to bring your robotic creations to life.

Robotics Kit

Dive into robotics with our Robotics Kit course, where you'll assemble and program robots using versatile kits. This hands-on experience blends engineering and coding, sparking innovation and problem-solving skills.

AI Kits

Explore the future of technology with our AI Kits course, where you'll build and program artificial intelligence models using easy-to-use kits. This course offers hands-on experience in AI, enhancing your skills in machine learning and automation.
